Campaign Overview

An organic social advocacy program designed to partner a lifestyle brand with micro-creators. By utilizing a standardized creative briefing framework, we generated a large asset base of authentic content that drove conversions.

Objective

Build social validation, accumulate authentic user-generated reviews, and scale brand trust across TikTok and Reels. The goal was to bypass typical corporate marketing in favor of high-performing creator channels.

Target Audience

Gen Z and young millennial buyers active in lifestyle subcultures. These users buy based on visual proof, peer reviews, styling guides, and authentic recommendations.

Strategy & Creative Direction

We engineered a structured creator onboarding guide specifying visual hook timings (first 3 seconds), visual text overlay formatting, and lighting setups, while leaving the narrative in the creator's voice to protect authenticity.

Channel & Platform Mix

TikTok was the primary channel for native reach (80%), supplemented by Instagram Reels for cross-promotional creator collaborations and product review catalogs (20%).

Learnings & Optimization

Videos that displayed creator reactions within the first 1.5 seconds saw a 45% increase in total watch retention. Using the creator's original audio rather than adding music tracks yielded a 14% higher link click rate.

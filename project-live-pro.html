PROJECT 03 / CREATOR GROWTH

LIVE PRO CAMPAIGN

Achieves a ~60% participation rate, compared to just 10–15% for typical campaigns.

Campaign Overview

A recurring growth campaign designed for Vietnam's top LIVE creators. Monthly research, gameplay mechanics, revenue targets, premium rewards, and recognition content worked as one system to sustain motivation and commercial performance.

Data-Driven Research: Analyzed monthly recap data, revenue trends, and behavioral patterns of top-tier creators in Vietnam to identify growth opportunities and optimization points.

  • Behavioral Analysis: Evaluated creator task completion rates, reward attractiveness, and psychological drivers to understand what motivates creators to push for higher targets.
  • Strategy & Gameplay Design: Developed monthly campaign strategies and task mechanics based on these insights to strengthen motivation, user desire, and monthly revenue.

Managed monthly vendor coordination to deliver custom-made merchandise for LIVE Pro winners. Each reward reflected the branding and personality of Vietnam's top creators, elevating the premium winner experience.

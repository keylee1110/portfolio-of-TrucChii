Campaign Overview

An editorial-driven digital positioning campaign engineered for a high-growth fashion retail brand. Operating in a highly competitive social space, the campaign focused on establishing cultural relevance, lifestyle integration, and organic viewer retention.

Objective

The primary objective was to expand top-of-funnel organic brand visibility among urban Gen Z audiences. We sought to shift public perception from a standard commercial store to a modern, culturally relevant style destination while maximizing social metrics and organic follower lists.

Target Audience

Digital-native fashion enthusiasts aged 18 to 25 living in metropolitan centers. These users are immune to traditional advertisements and seek visual authenticity, short-form storytelling, styling guides, and creator-led validation.

Strategy & Creative Direction

We bypassed traditional studio ad setups, opting for high-end visual brutalism. The content focused on raw, behind-the-scenes content styles, styling guides utilizing local cultural creators, desaturated color tones, and energetic visual pacing mapped directly to trending audio.

Channel & Platform Mix

A platform-native dual strategy: TikTok for high-reach trends, viral hooks, and creator challenges (70%); and Instagram Reels for polished aesthetic curations, product pairing layouts, and styling guides (30%).

Learnings & Optimization

Direct product placements and catalog ads had a 4x higher skip rate than organic creator styling challenges. Aligning visual transitions to exact musical tempos boosted average watch times by 22%, resulting in higher recommendations by platform algorithms.

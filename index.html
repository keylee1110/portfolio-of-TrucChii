04 / STRATEGIC DRILLDOWN

Case Study_ Product Launch Campaign

OBJECTIVE

Build awareness and drive product trial among Gen Z consumers.

STRATEGY

Combine organic short-form storytelling, micro-creator collaboration, paid boosting, and interactive social proof points.

CHANNELS

TikTok Organic, Instagram Reels, Meta Retargeting Ads, Creator Platforms.

The Execution

We mapped a three-tier content roadmap. First, we initiated **The Hook Phase**-producing native trend videos targeting student lifestyles. Second, we launched **The Credibility Phase**-briefing 35 lifestyle creators on specific product textures and benefits. Finally, **The Conversion Phase** utilized Meta Ads retargeting video viewers with trial coupon codes.

Key Learnings

Direct product pitches underperformed organic creator reactions by 140%. Creative pacing, specifically editing transitions in synchronization with trending tempos, boosted watch time rates by 22% overall.

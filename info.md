

Create a personal portfolio website for a **Digital Marketing Specialist / Campaign Strategist / Content Marketer** in a **minimalist editorial, premium black-and-white portfolio style**.

The website should be inspired by high-end creative portfolios with oversized typography, cinematic visuals, strong grid layout, and bold spacing, but the content must focus on **digital marketing performance, campaign results, brand growth, social media content, and data-driven achievements**.

The overall feeling should be **premium, confident, modern, strategic, and creative**, not too corporate and not too playful.

### Visual Style

Use a dominant **black background** with white and light gray typography.
Use oversized sans-serif typography, thin divider lines, large image blocks, editorial spacing, and asymmetrical layouts.

The design should feel like a mix between:

* luxury creative portfolio
* digital marketing case study website
* campaign archive
* brand strategy presentation
* social media performance dashboard

Avoid colorful startup-style UI, generic marketing templates, cartoon illustrations, and overly corporate designs.

### Website Structure

## 1. Hero Section

Create a strong first screen with:

* Name/logo on the left
* Quick links in the center: Home, Campaigns, Results, About, Contact
* Role/location on the right

Main headline:

**“Building Digital Campaigns that Turn Attention into Growth.”**

Alternative headline:

**“Where Creative Ideas Meet Measurable Marketing Impact.”**

Add a large personal brand/name in oversized typography, for example:

**“TrucLu™”**
or
**“Marketing Portfolio™”**

Add a cinematic marketing-related visual on the right, such as:

* social media campaign mockup
* phone screen with content preview
* brand photoshoot image
* campaign visual
* dashboard-style visual
* content production behind-the-scenes image

Add small micro text at the bottom:

* “© Digital Marketing Portfolio”
* “Campaign Strategy”
* “Content Performance”
* “Social Growth”

## 2. About / Positioning Section

Create a two-column section.

Left side: large portrait or lifestyle image.

Right side: strong headline:

**“A digital marketer focused on campaign strategy, content direction, social growth, and measurable brand impact.”**

Add short supporting copy:

**“I combine creative storytelling, performance thinking, and platform insights to build campaigns that connect with audiences and generate real business results.”**

Add outlined CTA button:

**CONTACT**

Below this section, add a row of mini statistic cards:

* **50+ Campaigns Managed**
* **10M+ Total Reach**
* **300K+ Engagements**
* **5+ Platforms**
* **20+ Brand Projects**

Use these as placeholder numbers that can be edited later.

**“Featured Campaigns©”**

## 4. Results / Metrics Section

Create a section focused on numbers.

Large title:

**“Results in Numbers.”**

Use large typography and clean statistic blocks.

Suggested metrics:

* **10M+** Total Campaign Reach
* **500K+** Total Engagements
* **40+** Campaigns Executed
* **120+** Short-form Videos Produced
* **8.5%** Average Engagement Rate
* **35%** Average Growth Rate

Use a layout similar to a premium dashboard, but keep it minimal and editorial.

Add thin lines, large numbers, and short explanations below each number.

## 5. Campaign Case Study Section

Create one detailed case study layout.

Title:

**“Case Study_ Product Launch Campaign”**

Structure:

* Background
* Marketing Objective
* Target Audience
* Strategy
* Creative Direction
* Channel Mix
* Execution
* Key Results
* Learnings

Use a mix of large text, campaign visuals, numbers, and small annotations.

Example content:

**Objective:** Build awareness and drive product trial among Gen Z consumers.
**Strategy:** Combine short-form storytelling, creator collaboration, paid boosting, and social proof.
**Execution:** Developed content pillars, produced short-form videos, collaborated with micro-creators, and optimized posting timing based on platform insights.
**Results:** 2.1M reach, 150K engagements, 6.8% engagement rate, and 32% increase in product inquiries.

## 8. Process Section

Large title:

**“Marketing Process.”**

02 Strategy
Campaign objective, key message, content angle, channel mix, and KPI planning.

03 Creative Direction
Visual concept, content hooks, video structure, captions, and creator briefing.

04 Execution
Publishing schedule, creator coordination, media boosting, and community response.

05 Optimization

06 Reporting

* Smooth scrolling
* Subtle fade-in and slide-up animations
* Image hover zoom effect
* Campaign cards reveal metrics on hover
* Sticky navigation
* Responsive mobile layout

### Final Output Goal

Create a premium digital marketing portfolio website that highlights:

* strong campaign visuals
* many campaign case studies
* clear marketing numbers
* measurable performance results
* social media growth
* content strategy
* influencer marketing
* brand storytelling
* professional but creative personal branding

The website should look visually similar to a high-end black-and-white creative portfolio, but the content must clearly communicate that this person is a **digital marketer who can create campaigns, grow brands, analyze numbers, and deliver measurable results**.

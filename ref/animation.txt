Create the testimonial section with a layered scroll effect.

Important:
This is not a normal static testimonial layout.
The section should behave like a parallax-style composition where the oversized background text stays fixed or sticky behind the content, while the testimonial cards move upward as the user scrolls.

Behavior:
- The large background word / title (such as “Reviews” or “Testimonial”) should remain visually anchored in place.
- It should stay behind all testimonial cards.
- The testimonial cards should scroll vertically through the section.
- As the page scrolls, the cards move upward naturally with the page, creating a layered depth effect.
- The result should feel like the testimonials are floating over a fixed editorial background.

Layout logic:
- Use one background layer for the huge text / visual.
- Use one foreground layer for the testimonial cards.
- The background layer should be fixed or sticky.
- The card layer should remain in the normal scroll flow or be animated slightly with scroll-based translateY.
- Keep strong negative space and asymmetrical card placement.

Visual feeling:
- Premium editorial portfolio
- Black canvas
- Oversized cropped white typography in the background
- Floating testimonial cards in front
- Smooth, subtle motion
- No obvious “techy” parallax effect
